Revenge

  • High Concept: Player needs to run towards the door to survive.
  • Player Experience Goals: Suspenseful cat and mouse kind of game.
  • Genre: A single sentence that places the game within a game genre or a hybrid of genres.
  • Game play: The player uses the arrow keys to move the character around the map. Player must avoid the enemies that are chasing after him.
  • Prototype goal: Spawning enemies ever few seconds at a random location on the map that will chase the player. I attempted to get the world bigger and have the camera follower the player but the camera just would not work.
  • Features: What are the unique aspects of your game that set it apart?
  • Setting/Premise: An underground dungeon/basement.
  • Story: Your character did something that angered the wrong kind of people and was kidnapped. The player was thrown into the basement full of enemies and they must try to escape alive.
  • Target Audience: Anyone really, no specific audience.
  • Play time: Less than a minute otherwise the screen will fill up with enemies.
  • Strategies: Move as fast as you can and probably stay close to the walls to get to the door.
  • Prototype Assets: Sprites are off of public domain sites.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Didn't really based it off of anything just did what I thought would be fun.
